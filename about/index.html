About Codatus

Codatus is built by Peter Novak, an independent developer based in the EU.

The project started as an attempt to solve a problem I kept hitting at previous orgs - answering "are our repos in a reasonable state?" without spending a week on spreadsheets every time someone asked. Tools that answer this question either don't exist for small platform teams or require deploying a full developer portal.

What we believe

Engineering standards work when they're measured, not when they're written into a wiki nobody reads. Codatus exists to make repository standards as visible as deploy frequency or PR throughput - just another metric your platform team can act on.

The scanner is open source on GitHub. Reach me at peter@codatus.com.
